About us

Welcome to Houseparty, a community on a mission to create an environment where gamers can thrive. Whether you are the new kid on the block or a seasoned veteran, Houseparty has a place for you. We provide a series of leagues, tournaments, and events catering to the needs and wishes of the community. When Houseparty first started, we were founded in the principle of becoming the “LCS for the rest of us”. Over time, that mission has changed here and there but our core value will always be to create a home for “the rest of us”. With the expansion in to different games, we are looking to create a home in every community out there. As of today, Houseparty hosts a variety of competitive and casual leagues for League of Legends and Rocket League and will be continuing to expand wherever we can. We are always welcoming new members, sponsors, ideas, and comments and have made it our priority to listen to what our community has to say. Houseparty is essentially what the players make of it, and with that in mind, Houseparty is whatever you look for it to be.
